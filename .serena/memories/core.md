# Project Core

- Project: `Helix-Workforce-Lifecycle-Compliance`.
- Authoritative root: `D:\\GitHub\\Helix-Workforce-Lifecycle-Compliance`.
- Scope: Taiwan Helix workforce lifecycle compliance and code-of-conduct governance for engineering, electromechanical, and lifting operations.
- The canonical model is HLC00–HLC14 plus relationship state, concurrent module instances, Helix assignment, overlay, case, and parallel work items.
- HLC00–HLC02 are entry modules; HLC03–HLC12 are concurrent and reentrant; HLC13 is voluntary resignation; HLC14 covers employer termination, retirement, shared closeout, and post-employment obligations.
- Employer, People Manager, Delivery Manager, Work Priority Owner, Technical Authority, Resource Owner, and HSE Authority are distinct roles. Each decision has one accountable role.
- Company grade, project role, competency, legal qualification, and technical authorization are distinct objects.
- Trial is an optional HLC06 subtype within an active relationship. Project release and Bench are HLC12 activities and do not end employment.
- HLC10 cases can coexist with any relationship state and require conflict routing, external paths where required, confidentiality, and anti-retaliation monitoring.
- Taiwan legal claims use current official government sources. Each evidence record carries one requirement, a status of `current | future-effective | pending`, and an explicit legal classification.
- The law catalog routes sources; the topic matrix routes issues; only the legal evidence register supports mandatory legal wording.
- Candidate artifacts remain schemas, not approved templates. Signature modes distinguish receipt, acknowledgement, consent, bilateral signature, authority approval, and external filing.
- The repository stores governance documents and empty schemas only, never named applicant, employee, contractor, consultant, dispatched-worker, intern, health, performance, or case data.
- Read `README.md`, `docs/README.md`, the canonical lifecycle model, the relevant module package, applicable gaps, and legal evidence. Follow `AGENTS.md`.
- This documentation model is not legal advice and does not establish operational or legal compliance.
